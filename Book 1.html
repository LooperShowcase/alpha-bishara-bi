Book One: Moon

Main Plot

The Dragon Prince Book 1: Moon sets off with the assassination of King Harrow, leaving his sons, Callum and Ezran, fleeing their kingdom with Rayla, an elf assassin who changes sides. Together, they embark on a perilous journey to return the Dragon Prince's egg to its mother, hoping to prevent a looming war between humans and elves. Along the way, they face various obstacles, including treacherous terrain, magical creatures, and human adversaries. The trio's bond strengthens as they navigate the challenges, and they gain unexpected allies. However, their success comes with the revelation of a sinister plot orchestrated by King Harrow's advisor, Viren, hinting at greater conflicts to come in the series.
