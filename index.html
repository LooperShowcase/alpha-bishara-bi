From BSH

"The Dragon Prince" is an animated series that follows the epic journey of two human princes, Callum and Ezran, and a Moonshadow elf named Rayla, as they strive to bring peace to their war-torn world. Created by Aaron Ehasz and Justin Richmond, the series debuted on Netflix in 2018 and quickly gained a dedicated fanbase. Drawing inspiration from traditional fantasy tropes while also introducing fresh twists, the show features rich world-building, complex characters, and intricate storytelling. The series explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the consequences of power, all set against the backdrop of a vibrant and magical world. With its compelling narrative and stunning animation, "The Dragon Prince" has captivated audiences of all ages and continues to garner praise for its creativity and depth.
