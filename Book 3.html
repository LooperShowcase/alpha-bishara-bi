Book Three: Sun


Main Plot

In "The Dragon Prince Book 3: Sun," Callum, Ezran, and Rayla continue their quest to restore peace in Xadia. As they confront new challenges and enemies, tensions between humans and elves reach a boiling point. With Viren's machinations escalating the conflict, the trio must navigate treacherous terrain and confront personal demons. As alliances are tested and secrets unearthed, they realize the true extent of the darkness threatening their world. To prevent war and restore balance, they must unite with allies and face their greatest challenge yet.
