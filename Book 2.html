Book Two: Sky


Main Plot

In "The Dragon Prince Book 2: Sky," Callum, Ezran, and Rayla journey deeper into Xadia, facing new dangers and personal struggles. As tensions between humans and elves escalate, fueled by Viren's schemes, the trio navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters formidable adversaries. With alliances tested and secrets revealed, they find themselves embroiled in a larger conflict threatening their world. To restore peace, they must confront darkness and unite with allies against looming war.
